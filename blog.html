Escape to the beautiful seaside of Halkidiki, Greece

05.12.2022

Escape to the beautiful seaside of Halkidiki, Greece with this stunning three-bedroom rental home from Paragon Associates. This property is the perfect getaway for families or groups of friends looking for a relaxing vacation by the sea.The house is located just a short walk from the beach, with breathtaking views of the Aegean Sea. The house is a three-bedroom, two-bathroom home that can comfortably accommodate up to six guests. The bedrooms are spacious, with comfortable beds and plenty of natural light. The living room is cozy and inviting, with a fireplace, comfortable seating, and a flat-screen TV. The kitchen is fully equipped with all the necessary appliances, as well as a dining area that seats six.Outside, the house boasts a terrace with a BBQ, a perfect place to enjoy the warm Mediterranean evenings. Guests can also enjoy the large garden, with a variety of outdoor seating areas, perfect for enjoying a morning coffee or an evening glass of wine.The property is ideally located in the area of Halkidiki, known for its beautiful beaches, clear waters and traditional villages. Guests can take a dip in the crystal clear sea or explore the many taverns and restaurants in the area. The property is also close to many tourist attractions such as the monasteries of Mount Athos and the ancient city of Nikiti.Paragon Associates is committed to making your stay as comfortable as possible, and our team is always on hand to assist you with any questions or concerns you may have. Book now and experience the beauty of Halkidiki for yourself!
